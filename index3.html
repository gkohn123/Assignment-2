My Family

My wife is a 4th year medical student at UNMC. We have two dogs Purkinje and Popliteal who are both shiba inu's.

Purkinje is the brown dog with the red border and is almost 4 years old. The black and tan dog with the yellow border is popliteal who is almost 2 years old. We enjoy going on walks and meeting other dogs and people. They love the snow and snuggling with anyone! We bought Purkinje from a store in Tucson,AZ and Popliteal from a breeder in western Nebraska.
